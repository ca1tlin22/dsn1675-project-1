Hi! My name is Caitlin Gagné. I’m a second year graphic design student at Algonquin College whose main interests include typography and illustration. Previously, I’ve taken a year of the Architectural Technician course and completed the Introduction to Concept Art program. Some of my favourite artists include Alphonse Mucha, Gustav Klimt and Laura Zombie. I am constantly inspired to create art and since I love to read I have been influenced by some of my authors are Stephen King, Neil Gaiman and Agatha Christie.
